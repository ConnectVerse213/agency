Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our Web3 marketing services

How do you ensure the quality of influencers in your network?

We've developed a rigorous vetting process over our 7 years in crypto marketing. We analyze engagement rates, audience quality, past campaign performance, and use proprietary tools to detect fake followers. Only about 10% of applicants make it into our network.

What types of projects do you work with?

We work with a wide range of Web3 projects including DeFi protocols, NFT collections, blockchain games, DAOs, and infrastructure projects. Our team has experience across all major blockchain ecosystems.

How do you measure campaign success?

We establish clear KPIs upfront based on your goals, whether that's community growth, engagement metrics, website traffic, or direct conversions. We provide detailed analytics reports showing exactly what results we've achieved.

Can you help with both global and regional marketing?

Absolutely. We have KOLs and community builders in all major regions including North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. We can tailor campaigns to specific geographic markets or run global initiatives.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing models depending on your needs - fixed project fees, monthly retainers, or performance-based pricing. Thanks to our bulk buying power with influencers, we can typically offer rates 20-30% below market.
